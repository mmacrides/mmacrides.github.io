Welcome to My Interactive Data Visualization

by Matt Macrides

This site provides a drill down story of personal income inequality distributions from 2012 - 2022. We start by looking at the United States as a whole, then we drill down into a United States cloropleth to show metric differences across States and then drill down into state specific metrics over the years. I used the Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) as my data source for the project: https://www.bea.gov/data/special-topics/distribution-of-personal-income.
