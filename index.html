Narrative Visualization - Income Distribution

by Matt Macrides

This interactive site presents a drill down story of several income distribution metrics from 2012 - 2022. We start by looking at the United States as a whole, then we drill down into a United States Choropleth, and finally, drill down into specific states. These three scenes allows users to see trends in income inequality over the years and across states. I used the Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) as my data source for the project: https://www.bea.gov/data/special-topics/distribution-of-personal-income.
