Income Distribution over the Years

This scene presents income distribution metrics in the United States over time. The "Eq. X/Y ratio" compares the income of individuals at the Xth percentile to the income of individuals at the Yth percentile. The larger the X or Y value, the more affluent. Additionally, the larger the final value, the more income disparity between groups. The Gini Coefficient is another measure of income inequality. A score of 0 means perfect equality, and a score of 1 means perfect inequality.
